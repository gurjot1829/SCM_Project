Egg Salad Crostini

Elevate your appetizer game with these easy Egg Salad Crostinis - creamy egg salad atop crunchy bread, finished with a sprinkle of chives for an added pop of flavor.


Ingredients for
12 crostinis

  • 12 slices baguette, 1/2 in (1 cm), cut on a slight diagonal
  • olive oil, to taste
  • kosher salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 4 eggs, medium-boiled, quartered lengthwise
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, finely chopped

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 425˚F (220˚C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Arrange the bread in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Brush each piece of bread with olive oil. Lightly sprinkle with salt. Bake for 12-14 minutes, until the edges are light golden brown. Let cool.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the Dijon mustard and mayonnaise.
  4. Spread a thin layer of Dijonnaise over each crostini. Top with 1 egg quarter and garnish with chives.
  5. Enjoy!
