Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Banana Protein Smoothie

Satisfy your sweet tooth and fuel your body with this dark chocolate peanut butter banana protein smoothie, made with cocoa powder, peanut butter, and frozen banana. It's the perfect breakfast or post-workout snack.


Ingredients for
2 servings

  • 1 ½ cups water(360 mL), or yogurt or milk of choice
  • ¼ cup peanut butter(60 g)
  • 1 scoop chocolate protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon dark chocolate cocoa powder
  • 2 bananas, frozen

Preparation

  1. Put all ingredients into a blender and mix until smooth.
  2. Enjoy!

Nutrition Info

  • Calories 522
  • Fat 19g
  • Carbs 49g
  • Fiber 8g
  • Sugar 21g
  • Protein 45g
