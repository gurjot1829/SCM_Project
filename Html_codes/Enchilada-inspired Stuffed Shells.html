Enchilada-inspired Stuffed Shells

These stuffed shells are filled with a delicious mix of ground beef, cheese, and enchilada sauce. Topped with more sauce and cheese, they're a tasty and filling dinner option that the whole family will love.


Ingredients for
5 servings

  • 2 teaspoons oil
  • ½ cup onion(75 g), diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ⅓ cup red bell pepper(35 g), diced
  • ⅓ cup green bell pepper(35 g), diced
  • ⅓ cup yellow bell pepper(35 g), diced
  • 1 lb ground beef(455 g)
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 1 lb jumbo shell(455 g), cooked
  • 1 cup enchilada sauce(250 g)
  • 1 cup mexican blend cheese(100 g)

Preparation

  4. Enjoy!
