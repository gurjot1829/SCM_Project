Honey Chipotle Chicken Poppers

Get ready to pop these Honey Chipotle Chicken Poppers into your mouth! These bite-sized delights are packed with sweet and spicy flavors that'll have you reaching for more.


Ingredients for
10 poppers

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 cup flour(125 g)
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • oil, for frying
  • Â¼ cup honey(85 g)
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon chipotle chili powder

Preparation

  1. Slice chicken breasts into bite-sized chunks.
  2. Combine the chicken with the salt, pepper, garlic powder, and chili powder, mixing until evenly seasoned.
  3. Place the flour and eggs into separate bowls.
  4. Dip a piece of chicken into the flour, then the egg, then back into the flour. Repeat with the rest of the chicken.
  5. Heat oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  6. Fry the chicken pieces until deep-golden brown and crispy.
  7. Set aside on a paper towel to drain
  8. In a pan over medium heat, mix together the honey, water, hot sauce, ketchup, and chipotle powder. Bring the sauce to a simmer, and cook for one minute.
  9. Toss the chicken in the sauce until evenly glazed.
  10. Enjoy!
