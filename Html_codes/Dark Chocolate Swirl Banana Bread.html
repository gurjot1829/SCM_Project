Dark Chocolate Swirl Banana Bread

This chocolate swirl banana bread that's both moist and flavorful. The creamy chocolate adds a decadent touch to classic banana bread for a deliciously satisfying treat.


Ingredients for
4 servings

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup greek yogurt(140 g)
  • ⅓ cup maple syrup(115 g)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour(125 g)
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ cup dark chocolate(45 g), chunks

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 325˚F (170˚C).
  2. Next, add the whole wheat flour and baking powder and mix until fully incorporated. Be careful not to over mix.
  3. In a medium, microwave-safe bowl, microwave the chocolate chunks in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until melted and smooth.
  4. Add a large scoop of the banana batter to the bowl of melted chocolate and fold until mixed well.
  5. Pour the banana batter and the chocolate batter alternately into a greased 9x5-inch (23x13 cm) bread pan. These do not need to be even layers.
  6. Using a knife or a spoon, gently swirl the batters together.
  7. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean from the center of the bread.
  8. Allow to cool for at least 15 minutes.
  9. Enjoy!
