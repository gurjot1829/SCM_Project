Halloween Candy Cookie Bars

These Halloween candy cookie bars are a fun and festive treat that are perfect for the spooky season. Made with your favorite Halloween candies, they are a sweet and indulgent dessert.


Ingredients for
9 servings

  • 1 cup white sugar(200 g)
  • 1 cup brown sugar(220 g)
  • 1 cup butter(230 g), melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups flour(250 g)
  • 2 cups assorted candy, chopped

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF (175ºC).
  2. Chop your Halloween candy into bite-size pieces.
  3. In a large bowl, mix sugars and melted butter, whisk until mix turns lighter (around 8 minutes).
  4. Mix in eggs and vanilla, then salt and flour until completely blended.
  5. Mix in the candy.
  6. Bake for 45-50 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool.
  8. Cut into squares.
  9. Enjoy!
