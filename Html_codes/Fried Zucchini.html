Fried Zucchini

This crispy and flavorful appetizer or side dish is made with fresh zucchini slices coated in a mixture of flour, cornmeal, and spices, then fried until golden brown. Serve with a side of ranch dressing for dipping.


Ingredients for
4 servings

  • 2 cups flour(250 g)
  • 6 eggs
  • Â½ cup milk(120 mL)
  • 2 cups italian style breadcrumbs(230 g)
  • 3 medium zucchinis
  • vegetable oil, for frying
  • salt
  • parmesan cheese
  • ranch dressing, for dipping

Preparation

  1. Set up three separate bowls. One with flour, one with egg mixed with milk, and one with bread crumbs.
  2. Cut zucchini in sticks or rounds.
  3. Dip in flour, then egg mix, then bread crumbs.
  4. Fry in oil over medium-high heat, turning a few times, until golden.
  5. Sprinkle with salt and parmesan. Serve immediately with ranch dip.
  6. Enjoy!
