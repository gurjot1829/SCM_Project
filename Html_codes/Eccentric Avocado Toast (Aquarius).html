Eccentric Avocado Toast (Aquarius)

This toast is a bold and unconventional masterpiece, just like a true Aquarius. With bananas and honey, it's a flavor adventure that'll have you seeing the world in a whole new way.


Ingredients for
1 serving

  • ½ avocado
  • kosher salt, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1 slice multigrain toast
  • ½ banana, sliced
  • honey, drizzle

Preparation

  1. Scoop the avocado into a medium bowl. Season with salt and add the lemon juice. Mash until smooth.
  2. Spread the avocado on the slice of toast. Top with the sliced banana and a drizzle of honey.
  3. Enjoy!

Nutrition Info

  • Calories 207
  • Fat 11g
  • Carbs 26g
  • Fiber 7g
  • Sugar 10g
  • Protein 2g
