Chapati And Jam Sandwich

Experience a delightful twist on a classic sandwich with this Chapati and Jam recipe. This sweet and satisfying treat combines soft, homemade chapati with your favorite jam for a scrumptious snack that'll have you saying "Yum-O!"


Ingredients for
2 servings

  • 2 chapatis, cooked
  • 1 tablespoon cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon jam
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Preparation

  1. On a chapati, generously spread cream cheese and jam. Cover with the other chapati.
  2. Add ghee on a hot pan and cook the chapati sandwich on both sides. Cut and serve.
  3. Enjoy!

Nutrition Info

  • Carbs 28g
  • Fiber 2g
