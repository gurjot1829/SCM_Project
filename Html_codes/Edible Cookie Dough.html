Edible Cookie Dough


Ingredients for
1 serving

  • ¼ cup flour(30 g)
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate chips

Preparation

  1. Mix together granulated sugar, brown sugar, salt, milk, and vanilla in a small bowl.
  2. Once combined, add in flour and mix.
  3. Then, add the butter and fold until it becomes a dough. Finally, add chocolate chips
  4. Enjoy!
