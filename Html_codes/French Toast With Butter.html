French toast with butter

Bonjour to this classic French Toast with Butter recipe that'll have you saying "Oui, s'il vous pla√Æt!" With a golden, crispy exterior and a soft, custardy center, this breakfast delight is perfect for a weekend brunch or a cozy morning in.


Ingredients for
2 servings

  • 3 slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons powdered milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essense
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Preparation

  1. Cut bread into halves and set aside.
  2. Crack eggs into a bowl and to them, add sugar, milk, vanilla essence and whisk.
  3. Heat vegetable oil and butter in a skillet on low heat. Once the butter is melted, dip bread slices in egg mixture and toast in the skillet until golden-brown.
  4. Serve with whipped cream, powdered sugar, and maple syrup.
  5. Enjoy!
