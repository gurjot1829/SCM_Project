Pumpkin Spice Coffee

Embrace the cozy vibes of fall with this Pumpkin Spice Coffee that'll warm your soul. This delightful concoction combines the rich flavours of coffee and pumpkin spice, making it the perfect morning pick-me-up or afternoon treat.


Ingredients for
2 servings

  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons pumpkin puree
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • whipped cream

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a medium sauce pot and heat through, making sure not to boil, stirring occasionally.
  2. Serve hot, or over ice!
  3. Top with whipped cream (optional)
  4. Enjoy!
