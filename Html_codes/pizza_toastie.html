Pizza Toastie

For a lazy night in, try this quick and delicious pizza toastie stuffed with cheese and pepperoni.


Ingredients for
1 servings

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 tablespoons marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 6 pieces pepperoni
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Preparation

  1. Top one piece of bread with marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni.
  2. Cover with a second piece of bread and butter both slices of bread from edge to edge.
  3. Place in preheated toasties machine and cook until bread is golden brown. (If the toastie is not browning evenly, flip half way through).
  4. Remove from toastie and slice in half.
  5. Enjoy!

Nutrition Info

  • Calories 56g
  • Fat 2g
  • Carbs 34g
  • Fiber 3g
  • Sugar 1g
  • Protein 23g
