Granola Bowl

Enjoy a breakfast classic with this homemade granola. Crunchy and nutty, this recipe is perfect for sprinkling over yogurt or enjoying on its own as a nutritious snack.


Ingredients for
8 servings

  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • greek yogurt
  • fresh berry

Preparation

  1. Preheat the oven to 425F (220C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the oats, almonds, cinnamon, salt, coconut oil, vanilla extract, maple syrup, and honey until well-combined.
  3. Transfer the mixture onto the prepared baking sheet and spread in an even layer.
  4. Bake the granola for 20 minutes, until golden brown. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 10 minutes.
  5. Break up the granola into chunks with your hands.
  6. Serve with Greek yogurt and fresh berries, if desired. Leftovers will keep in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 1 month.
  7. Enjoy!
