Golgappas


Ingredients for
7 servings

  • 1 cup fine semolina(125 g)
  • 1 cup all purpose flour(125 g), maida
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, add semolina, all-purpose flour, salt and a pinch of baking soda. Mix together and knead the dough with water. The dough consistency should be medium neither too hard nor too soft.
  2. Rest the dough for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Roll the dough out until thin, rolling from all sides to create a large circle. Then, take a small round cookie cutter and cut circles, each around 4-5 cm.
  4. Cover the shaped pooris with a damp cloth and do the same for the rest of the dough.
  5. Heat the oil in a deep frying pan and fry the pooris on medium flame. While frying, gently press the pooris with the back of a ladle, allowing the dough to puff up. Flip the poori and fry until both sides are crispy and golden.
  6. Transfer the fried poori to a plate or bowl lined with paper towels.
  7. Either serve right away or let cool and store in an airtight container to snack on later!
  8. Enjoy!
