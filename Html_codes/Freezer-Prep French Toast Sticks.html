Freezer-Prep French Toast Sticks

Do something for your future self and make these French toast sticks that can be prepped ahead of time and stored in the freezer. They can be quickly reheated in the oven or microwave, making them an easy and tasty breakfast option for busy mornings.


Ingredients for
36 sticks

  • 1 loaf texas toast
  • 7 eggs
  • 1 cup milk(240 mL)
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • salt, to taste
  • cooking spray

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180˚C).
  2. Spray a large baking sheet with cooking spray.
  3. Stack and slice the Texas toast into thirds.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, butter, vanilla, cinnamon, salt, and sugar.
  5. Dunk each strip of bread into the mixture, allow the excess to drip off, then place on prepared baking sheet. Repeat with remaining strips
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway.
  7. Remove from oven and serve
  8. Enjoy!
