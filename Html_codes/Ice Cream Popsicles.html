Ice Cream Popsicles

Chill out and cool down with these homemade frozen treats, no molds needed! Creamy, luscious ice cream is layered into a loaf pan to mimic the classic Neapolitan trio. Simply insert wooden sticks, then cut into individual pops and serve. Perfect for a refreshing summer snack or a fun dessert, these popsicles are sure to bring smiles to faces young and old!


Ingredients for
10 servings

  • 2 cups chocolate ice cream(295 g)
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream(295 g)
  • 2 cups strawberry ice cream(295 g)
  • wooden ice pop stick

Preparation

  1. Add 2 cups (295 g) of softened chocolate ice cream to a tin foil tub, and freeze for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Layer with 2 cups (295 g) of softened vanilla ice cream, and freeze for another 15-20 minutes.
  3. Top with 2 cups (295 g) of softened strawberry ice cream and freeze for 1-2 hours.
  4. Lastly, add 10 popsicle sticks to the frozen rub. Cut the sides of the tub at appropriate distances.
  5. Slice the popsicles and serve.
  6. Enjoy!
