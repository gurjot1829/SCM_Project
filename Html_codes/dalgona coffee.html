Dalgona Coffee

This trendy coffee drink features a whipped mixture of instant coffee, sugar, and milk, for a creamy and frothy caffeinated treat.


Ingredients for
serving

  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • milk

Preparation

  1. Add coffee, sugar, and water into a bowl or cup, and whip until it reaches a meringue like consistency!
  2. Take a cup of milk & spoon whipped coffee on it
  3. Enjoy!
