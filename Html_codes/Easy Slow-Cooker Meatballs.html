These flavorful meatballs are made with a combination of ground beef and pork, breadcrumbs, and spices, then cooked in a slow cooker with a delicious tomato-based sauce.


Ingredients for
4 servings

  • 32 oz frozen meatball(900 g)
  • 2 cups pure grape jelly(500 mL)
  • 2 cups Heinz chili sauce(455 mL)
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chili flakes
  • fresh parsley, optional garnish: chives/green onions

Preparation

  1. Add the first five ingredients into the base of a slow cooker. Mix until well combined.
  2. Cover and cook on high for 3-4 hours, stirring halfway through cooking. Keep covered and on the warm setting until ready to serve.
  3. Garnish with parsley or chives or green onions if desired and serve.
  4. Enjoy!
