Fried Chocolate Mochi Balls

These fried chocolate mochi balls are a little ball of happiness. The chewy mochi exterior filled with rich chocolate is the ultimate indulgence.


Ingredients for
8 balls

  • ¾ cup sweet rice flour(110 g)
  • ½ tablespoon cornstarch
  • ½ cup water(120 mL)
  • chocolate chip
  • oil, for frying

Preparation

  1. Combine the sweet rice flour, cornstarch and water in a mixing bowl.
  2. Turn out on table and knead to form a ball of dough.
  3. Grab a ping pong size of dough, create a well.
  4. Fill with chocolate chips. Wrap the dough around the chocolate chips and form into a ball.
  5. NOTE: Make sure to seal well and push out any air pockets. If you don’t push out air pockets, they will pop and burst will frying.
  6. Deep fry until the outside is crispy and the mochi balls start floating.
  7. Enjoy!
