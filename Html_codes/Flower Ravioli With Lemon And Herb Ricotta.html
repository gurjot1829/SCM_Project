Flower Ravioli With Lemon And Herb Ricotta

Add edible flowers to your pasta dough to give your dish a spring touch and aesthetic.


Ingredients for
8 servings

    PASTA

  • 2 cups 00 flour(250 g), plus more for dusting
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

    • HERB AND LEMON RICOTTA FILLING

  • 1 lb whole milk ricotta(250 g), strained and pressed overnight
  • 4 lemons, zested
  • 3 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese(125 g)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • basil pesto or sauce of choice, for serving

Preparation

  4. Enjoy!
